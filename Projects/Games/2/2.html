Space Bear Romance


Fall 2020



HOW TO: WASD for the player on the left. Arrows for the player on the right. One of the bears can double jump, and the other has super speed.

DESCRIPTION: This game was a submission to the Beginners Circle Jam #3. I made it in two weeks. The premise is that two lovers have been separated and need to be reunited. Each level consists of progressively harder parkour.

TOOLS: Unity C#, Piskel
