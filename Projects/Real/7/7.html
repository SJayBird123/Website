Knight Movement App


Fall 2022



PURPOSE: To visualize how a knight moves on a chessboard.

HOW TO: Drag the knight anywhere on the board and see how many moves it would take to get to each square.

DESCRIPTION: I made this interactive tool after seeing a similar Reddit post. Aside from the novelty of seeing the data displayed like this, seeing knight movement can be helpful for beginner to intermediate players to build visualization skills.

TOOLS: Unity C#, Java, Piskel

LINKS: Itch.io Link, Reddit Post
