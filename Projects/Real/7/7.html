Knight Movement App


Fall 2022



A tool designed to visualize how a knight moves on a chess board. Drag the knight anywhere on the board and see how many moves it would take to get to each square. Aside from the novelty of seeing the data displayed like this, seeing knight movement can be useful for beginer to intermediate players for building visualization skills. Itch.io Link Reddit Post
