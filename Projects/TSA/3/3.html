Cats in Egypt


Winter 2022



HOW TO: WASD keys for movement. Left-Click to attack. W and Left-Click to jump attack.

DESCRIPTION: This game was a submission to the TSA Video Game Design event in 2022. This game follows the role of cats in ancient societies, with historical descriptions for each level.

TOOLS: Unity C#, Piskel
