The Great Chase


Winter 2021



HOW TO: WASD keys for movement. Left-Click to attack.

DESCRIPTION: This game was a submission to the TSA Video Game Design event in 2021. The premise is that you are scientist Frank N. Stein and must journey to retrieve your mythical monsters.

TOOLS: Unity C#, Piskel
